STRONGHOLD® MARKS 25 YEARS

Squire Bike Locks

In 1963 we launched the first range of cycle locks in the UK. We were ahead of our time and have continued to refine our products using modern innovative technology to create some of the toughest locks available, independently attack tested with proven theft resistance.

We stand by our reputation for long-lasting toughness by offering a personal 10-year guarantee from John Squire on our entire Bike Lock catalogue.

Choose from our unrivalled selection including Stronghold® high security bike locks, heavy duty Padlock & Chain Sets, unique Snaploks™, D-Locks, Integrated Chain Locks, compact Folding Locks, key and combination Cable Locks and our inigma™ smart technology range. Peace of mind At Home or On-The-Go, we’ve got you covered.
